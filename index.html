Upcoming Courses

Hexify

A 6-month duration course that is separate for both classes 11th and 12th. The Hexify-A covers class 12th syllabus as well as the competitive syllabus for IIT-JEE exam. Same goes for Hexify-B which covers the syllabus of class 11th. Taught by Professor Ramamurthy, Professor Kapoor and Professor Blue. Course starting on October 1, 2017.

Registrations open for Scholaria

Scholarship Scheme

We value the talents of the young minds and provide scholarships to those students who have a weak economic base but possess the talent to achieve. The scholarship is provided to the top 20 rank holders in the Scholarship exam. The Scholarship exam, Scholaria, will be held on coming sunday viz. September 10, 2017. Registrations open!
